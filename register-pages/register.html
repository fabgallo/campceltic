[frame_right]http://www.campceltic.ca/wpimages/register_mainform.gif[/frame_right]It's time to register! We recommend you first download our registration form. It has all the information you need regarding Summer Camp, Sports Camp and Leadership Camp including dates and prices. Campers registering for more than one session and families with three or more campers should contact Celtic for a quote on fees. Campers attending consecutive camp sessions may stay over free of charge between sessions. Once we receive your registration we will send you a complete list of what campers need to bring with them, times for camp drop-off and pick-up, and a receipt for income tax purposes. Your child’s camp fees may be tax-deductible. You have two options for registering your child:

Option 1: Register Online

Our online registration form allows you to quickly and easily register your child for camp. Once you confirm your submission, payment is made securely with a Visa or MasterCard credit card. If you have received a quote on fees, would like to follow a payment plan as indicated below, or use a different payment method, you are required to register by mail. Please fill out as much of the form as you can. We will require any missing information before your child arrives at Camp Celtic. [small_button link="http://campceltic.ca/register/online"]Register Online[/small_button]

Option 2: Register by Mail

You can also register your child by filling out the registration form and mailing it back to us.

Arrival, Departure and bus info

Campers should arrive at Celtic between 1:00 p.m. and 3:00 p.m. on the starting day of each session and be picked up from camp between 10:00 a.m. and 12:00 noon on the closing day of camp. Exception: Session 8 ends on a Friday evening and campers should be picked up between 4:00 p.m. and 6:00 p.m. Celtic will provide free bus transportation to/from Guelph and Woodstock at the end of Session 2 AND 4, at the start of Session 3 AND 5; and at the end of Session 8.
